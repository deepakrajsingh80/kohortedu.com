2. Why Choose Purdue?

  • Top 10 Engineering School in the US at public university price
  • More astronauts than any other university — NASA's favorite recruitment ground
  • 2,500+ Indian students with a strong, tight-knit STEM community
  • Affordable tuition for the quality offered — best ROI among top engineering schools
  • World's largest academic propulsion lab — Zucrow Labs
  • Strong industry recruiting from Boeing, Lockheed Martin, SpaceX, Amazon, and more
  • Safe, affordable college town — West Lafayette is built around students

Insider Tip
Purdue is known as the "Poor Man's MIT" — offering comparable engineering quality at a fraction of the cost. If you're an Indian student with a strong STEM background and a tight budget, Purdue should be at the top of your list.

Standardized Test Details

GRE: Verbal (130-170) + Quantitative (130-170) + AWA (0-6). Total 305+ recommended. 315+ makes you competitive. For Aerospace Engineering, aim for 320+. GRE is valid for 5 years. Fee: $220 (approx Rs 18,500).

IELTS: Listening, Reading, Writing, Speaking — each scored 0-9. Overall 6.5 minimum, 7.0+ recommended. Valid for 2 years. Fee: Rs 16,250.

Required Documents

  • 10th & 12th Mark Sheets and Certificates
  • Bachelor's Degree Certificate
  • All Semester Mark Sheets / Transcript
  • GRE Score Card
  • IELTS / TOEFL Score Card
  • Passport (valid for 6+ months)
  • Statement of Purpose (SOP)
  • 3 Letters of Recommendation (LOR)
  • Resume / CV
  • Financial Proof ($45,000+ in bank)
  • Application Fee Receipt ($75)
  • Backlog Certificate (if applicable)
Important: Purdue requires proof of funds showing at least $45,000 (approx Rs 37.5 Lakhs) for the first year. This can be a bank statement, education loan sanction letter, or sponsor letter.

5. Application Deadlines

Intake Application Deadline Decision Date Classes Begin
Fall (Primary) January 1 March 15 August
Spring September 1 October 15 January
Pro Tip
Apply for Fall intake. Fall is the primary intake with more seats, more funding opportunities, and better assistantship chances. Spring intake has limited seats and fewer scholarship options. Submit your application by December 1 to be safe — don't wait until the January 1 deadline.

Application Process

  1. Create an account on the Purdue Graduate School Application Portal
  2. Select your program (Aerospace, CS, Mechanical, etc.)
  3. Fill in personal and academic details
  4. Upload all required documents (transcripts, SOP, LORs, resume)
  5. Report GRE and IELTS scores (use Purdue's institution code: 1631)
  6. Pay the $75 application fee
  7. Submit and track your application status online

Cost-Saving Tips

  • Graduate Assistantship: Covers full tuition + $1,500-2,500/month stipend. Apply with your admission.
  • Part-time work: 20 hrs/week on campus. Earn $800-1,200/month.
  • Summer internship: CPT allows paid internships. Average: $5,000-8,000/month.
  • Shared accommodation: Sharing an apartment reduces rent by 40-50%.
  • Cook your own food: Indian groceries available. Cooking saves $200-300/month.

Indian Food & Groceries

West Lafayette has excellent Indian food options: Chaat House, Bombay Cafe, India Mahal. For groceries, Patel Brothers stocks all Indian spices, lentils, and snacks. Many Indian students cook together and share meals.

Indian Student Community

  • Purdue Indian Students Association (ISA) — 500+ members, organizes all major Indian festivals
  • Bharati — Indian classical dance and music group
  • Purdue Cricket Club — Regular matches and tournaments
  • Hindi Cinema Club — Bollywood movie nights

Festivals Celebrated

India Night, Diwali, Holi, Garba — All major Indian festivals are celebrated on campus with hundreds of attendees. India Night is the biggest event, featuring Bollywood dances, Indian food stalls, and cultural performances.

Weather

Winter: -10°C to 5°C (Nov-Mar) — Very cold, snow common. Buy a heavy winter jacket.
Summer: 20°C to 30°C (Jun-Aug) — Warm and pleasant.
Tip: Indian students often struggle with the first winter. Invest in good winter gear before you arrive.

Safety

West Lafayette is a very safe college town with low crime rates. The campus is well-lit and has a 24/7 security patrol. Purdue also offers a safe ride service for late-night travel.

Weekend Getaways

  • Indianapolis — 1 hour drive. State capital, good shopping and dining.
  • Chicago — 2 hours drive. Major city, Indian grocery hub, airport.
  • Indiana Dunes National Park — 1.5 hours. Beautiful lakeside beaches.

10. Application Checklist

Before You Apply (3-6 months prior)

  • Take GRE and aim for 310+ (315+ for Aerospace)
  • Take IELTS and aim for 7.0+
  • Request transcripts from your university
  • Get 3 LORs from professors/employers
  • Draft your Statement of Purpose (SOP)
  • Prepare your Resume/CV
  • Arrange financial documents ($45K+ proof)

Application Steps

  • Visit purdue.edu/gradschool and create an account
  • Select your program (Aerospace / CS / Mechanical)
  • Fill in all personal and academic details
  • Upload transcripts, SOP, LORs, and resume
  • Send GRE scores (code: 1631) and IELTS scores
  • Pay $75 application fee
  • Submit application before January 1 (Fall) or September 1 (Spring)
  • Track application status online

After Acceptance

  • Accept offer and pay enrollment deposit
  • Apply for I-20 (Certificate of Eligibility)
  • Pay SEVIS fee ($350)
  • Schedule F-1 visa interview at US Embassy
  • Prepare visa documents (I-20, financial proof, SOP)
  • Attend visa interview
  • Book flight tickets
  • Arrange temporary accommodation
  • Attend Purdue orientation (mandatory)
Visa Wait Times: US visa interview appointments in India can have wait times of 3-6 months. Book your appointment as soon as you receive your I-20. Do not delay.
