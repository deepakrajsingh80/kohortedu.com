## Disclaimer

All figures on Kohortconnect are:
1. **Sourced from official government and university websites** where available
2. **Representative averages** — actual costs vary by institution, program, and location
3. **Updated regularly** — currency rates and living costs are reviewed quarterly
4. **Converted to INR** using RBI reference rates at the time of display

> **Note:** Tuition fees and living costs are subject to annual increases. Visa policies change based on government decisions. Always verify current figures with the official university or immigration website before making decisions. Kohortconnect data is for guidance only.
